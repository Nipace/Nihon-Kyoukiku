About Us

Nihon Kyouiku Nepal Consultancy And Training Center

Our Office

Nihon Kyouiku Nepal Consultancy and Training Centre Pvt. Ltd., a consulting company working only for Japan. It was established on July 29, 2019. The sole purpose of this company is to provide brief description about history, culture, manners, rules and regulations of Japan. We aim to provide particular counseling about “Student Visa”, “Working Visa Counseling”, “Training Visa Counseling” and new Working (“Specified Skilled Worker”) Visa.

The objective of this company is to provide immense information related to the individual visa procedures with documentation. We believe in quality and tends to provide best services to our clients.
