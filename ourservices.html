Our services

Nihon Kyouiku Nepal Consultancy And Training Center

Japanese Language Classes

We are running morning, day and evening Japanese language classes for students, working, training Intern-ship and specified skilled worker from Sunday to Friday. We have excellent Japanese language teacher returned from Japan with good teaching experience.

Skill Test Preparatory Classes

We also provide skill test preparatory classes. We have excellent teachers in various field of skill test for “Specified Skill Worker”.

Document Guidance

We provide excellent expertise for documentation of various Visa applications. From the very beginning of counselling till the visa success period, we give each and every individual a proper documentation plan and formats. We guide every details about each and every field of visa documents.

Visa processing

Our visa processing includes following Visa:

  1. Student Visa
  2. Working Visa counselling(Direct)
  3. Traininig Visa Counselling(3 years)
  4. Internship Visa Counselling
  5. "Specified Skilled Worker" Visa Counselling

Test Preparation

We provide the preparation classes for JLPT N5, N4, N3, N2 and NAT TEST 5Q, 4Q, 3Q, 2Q every Sunday. We also provide mock-test for skill test preparation every month.

Interview preparation

We provide the excellent practical knowledge for our clients. Interview is one of the main key to obtain your visa. We make you confidence to face the various kind of interviews during the process.

Career planning

During counseling, we discuss each individual about their future plan. We provide various opinion in various field of careers. We guide and provide you a proper planning regarding your career advancement in Japan.

Japanese Translation　

We also provide translating services of all kinds of JAPANESE-NEPALI, NEPALI-JAPANESE, JAPANESE- ENGILSH, and ENGLISH-JAPANESE which is unofficial Translation.
